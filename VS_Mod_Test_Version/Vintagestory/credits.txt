Game engine:
==============
Originally based on ManicDigger (http://manicdigger.github.io/). About 95% of it has been rewritten

Open source libraries:
==============
OpenTK
OpenAL
SharpZipLib
Open.Nat
SharpAvi
Tavis.JsonPatch (modified)
Mono.Nat
Cairo Graphics
Newtonsoft.Json
SQLite3
...and more. See Lib, Lib32 and Lib64 folder for a full list
